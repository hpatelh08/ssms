Frequently Asked Questions

What are the visiting hours?
Regular campus tours are conducted every Saturday from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM. For weekday visits, prior appointment booking is required. School timings are 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM on weekdays. Please contact the admissions office to schedule your visit.
How can I schedule a campus visit?
You can schedule a campus visit by calling our admissions office at +91-98765-43210, emailing info@smartschool.edu, or filling out the booking form on our website. We recommend booking 1-2 days in advance, especially for group visits.
Is there an admission inquiry process?
Yes! Admission inquiries can be made in person at our admissions office, via phone (+91-98765-43210), or email (admissions@smartschool.edu). We provide comprehensive information about admission criteria, fee structure, and available scholarships during the consultation.
Can I meet with the Principal or staff members?
Meeting with senior staff can be arranged through the admissions office. Due to academic commitments, such meetings require advance notice (at least 3-5 business days). We ensure all inquiries receive proper attention and timely responses.
Are there any special programs or scholarships?
We offer merit-based scholarships, sports scholarships, and need-based financial assistance programs. Details about eligibility criteria and application procedures can be obtained from the admissions office or our website.
What facilities are available on campus?
Our campus features state-of-the-art classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs, a modern library, sports complex, auditorium, cafeteria, and medical facility. All facilities are accessible and maintain highest safety standards.
